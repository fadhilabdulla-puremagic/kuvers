Valves

Founded in 2018 as a subsidiary of the Kürvers Group, Kürvers Valves was established to address specific customer needs and requirements.

With a legacy spanning nearly 60 years and a team of over 100 employees worldwide, the Kürvers Group is a respected global leader in its industry, particularly in piping and related products. Our extensive references and strong reputation reflect our commitment to excellence. We maintain a global presence, with local offices and sales representatives serving customers across more than 15 countries

Kürvers Valves specializes in the trade of industrial valves, as well as providing comprehensive planning, processing, and project management services for international EPC contractors, power plants, oil and gas infrastructure, and the chemical industry

Kürvers Valve Products

At Kürvers Valves, we provide products precisely tailored to customer specifications and optimized for their applications. Our extensive portfolio includes a wide range of designs, materials, sizes, compatible accessories, and specialized solutions, along with fastening materials, pipe sections, and seals. This diverse selection and high product availability make us the ideal partner, whether for single-valve needs or large-scale projects. We deliver exceptional quality with a strong price-performance balance, ensuring seamless integration of all products from our manufacturers.

Our dedicated quality management team ensures all work meets the highest standards, prioritizing both quality and safety. Customers benefit from cost-effective solutions that are engineered for reliable, long-term performance, minimized maintenance requirements, and high profitability.
