About us

Founded in Düsseldorf in 1966, Kürvers is an international operating group of companies and a proven specialist for pipeline projects in the petrochemical and energy industries.

With over 55 years of experience, we excel in procuring materials globally for complex projects, ensuring unmatched delivery through trusted supplier relationships. Our commitment to reliability, integrity, and collaboration drives us to exceed expectations and meet every client's unique project requirements.
